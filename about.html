A pleasure to meet you, I'm Jin / 진 [she/her]

Korean-Canadian writer, storyteller, and communications strategist with experience across journalism, content, and operations. Based in Toronto.

I write journalism, brand copy, press materials, fundraising campaigns, and content strategy. Usually, I’m moving between editorial thinking, client-facing communication, and operations (and trying to do it all without making a fuss).

I’m the first in my family to graduate from university, and I grew up fostering dogs and cats, so I’ve always had a soft spot for responsibility that looks a lot like care. I studied political science and philosophy before journalism, which makes sense if you like overthinking things on purpose.

I’ve worked with beauty startups, established businesses, and agency clients, and I like the range that comes with that. I’m also the kind of person who gets weirdly into Sudoku, thinks change is underrated, and welcomes breadth without losing precision.

I do more than write. But writing is always where I start.
